Basic Biscuits



Description:


This is an easy homemade biscuit recipe with baking powder used as the leavening. The biscuits turn out perfectly soft and flaky on the inside every time.


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Shortening
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Knead and Roll
  3. Cut and Bake
